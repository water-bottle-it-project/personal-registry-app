Team 55 IT Project : Team Structure

Created by Calvin, last modified by Ian Chen on Oct 23, 2022

Task Delegation

Considering the fact that we are assessed on an individual basis, in order to fairly split tasks among each member, we have decided to implement a team structure where everyone is in charge of a certain aspect. We roughly split the project into three areas: frontend, backend and testing. The lead in each area may split the task and assign them to each individual. If any team member disagrees with the split, he may bring up the issue with the project manager and let the project manager make the final decision.

  • If tasks are difficult, large, or require coordination e.g. data-fetching, multiple people can tackle a task a together.

  • These roles can change during sprints - to be decided during sprint retrospective!

  • Everyone can (and should) take tasks across a variety of areas - frontend, backend, devops/testing, even if they lead a particular section to gain experience.

Task Review

Every time someone finishes a task, he needs to move the ticket to the review section. Once the lead has commented “approved”, the ticket can be moved to done.
